Submission Instructions:

Submitted papers must be neither previously published nor under review by another workshop, conference or journal.

Submissions must be written in English and adhere to the ACM Proceedings Format. We solicit two types of contributions as follows:

  • Research papers (max. 10 pages): Work presenting novel ideas, practical solutions to RSSE.
  • Vision and position papers (max. 6 pages): Work in progress, visionary papers, and industrial experiennce.

Papers should use the standard ACM Template, available at ACM Article Template .

To submit your paper, please follow below link to the submission site:

Paper Submission Site

Note that at least one of the authors of each paper accepted for presentation in EASE 2021 must be registered.
